Directions

Move all the Shinjis until they are alone and overwhelmingly depressed. Only Just Right Shinjis can be moved. Click the Shinji to select him, then select a new square for him to go to.

Shinji can be in different states depending on the tiles he is adjacent to:

  • Overwhelmingly Depressed if he is surrounded by no Shinjis.
  • Bored if he is surrounded by just one other Shinji.
  • Just Right (for now) if he is surrounded by two other Shinjis.
  • Embarassed if he is surrounded by three other Shinjis.
  • Annoyed if he is surrounded by four other Shinjis.

Lovingly ripped off from Silent World by Hojamaka.
